ABOUT

I'm a creative technologist specializing in live visuals, shader programming, and audio-visual synthesis. As a VJ, I perform live visuals using VDMX software, creating immersive experiences that blend music and visual art.

My work explores the intersection of code, mathematics, and art through real-time generative systems. I create integrated audio-visual pieces that combine modular synthesis with custom GLSL shaders and procedural graphics.

Currently transitioning into full-time creative work, focusing on pushing the boundaries of real-time visual programming and exploring new territories in generative art.
